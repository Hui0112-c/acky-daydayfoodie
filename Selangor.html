Main Dishes

Hokkien Mee

Hokkien Mee is a popular noodle dish from the Hokkien community. In Selangor and Kuala Lumpur, it is cooked with thick yellow noodles stir-fried in dark soy sauce along with pork, prawns, squid and crispy pork lard.

Curry Laksa

Curry Laksa is a noodle soup made with a spicy coconut milk curry broth. It can be served with either yellow egg noodles or bee hoon or both, depending on your preference. The dish comes with tofu puffs, chicken, prawns, bean sprouts and boiled egg to make it rich and flavorful.

Bakuteh

Bak Kut Teh is a Chinese herbal soup made by simmering pork ribs with a blend of herbs, spices and garlic to create a rich and aromatic broth. It is often enjoyed with rice, fried dough sticks and sometimes served with tofu, mushrooms or vegetables.

Mee Rebus Klang

Mee Rebus Klang is a noodle dish from Klang. It is served with thick yellow noodles in a spicy-sweet gravy made from potatoes, shrimp, and spices. It also usually garnished with boiled eggs, tofu and green onions.

Satay Kajang

Satay Kajang is a famous supper dish from Kajang, Selangor. It is made of skewered meat, usually chicken, beef or lamb, marinated with spices and grilled over charcoal. The dish is served with peanut sauce, ketupat, onions and cucumber.

Sha Ba

"Sha Ba" is a colloquial term for a peppery soup. It is usually made with pork belly and bittergourd. This dish is cherished for its balanced spicy, savory and slightly bitter flavors.

Sesame Ball

Image source: Rasa Malaysia Website


Sesame Ball is a snack made from glutinous rice flour filled with sweet paste such as red bean or mung bean. It is coated with sesame seeds and deep-fried until golden and crispy. The snack is loved for its crunchy exterior with soft and sweet filling.

Cendol Durian

Image source: Cendol Durian Runtuh's Google Review


Cendol Durian is a popular Malaysian dessert made with green rice flour jelly, coconut milk, palm sugar syrup and topped with fresh durian. It is loved for its creamy texture with its rich and sweet durian flavor.
