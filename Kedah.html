Laksa Kedah

A popular dish in Kedah, laksa Kedah is a must-have whenever you come here. The dish consists of rice noodles in fish-based gravy with tangy asam flavour. It is garnished with cucumber and onions as well as fragranced herbs such as daun kesum. For laksa Kedah, it is served with variety of finely-sliced ulam, like daun selom, ulam raja and pucuk gajus.

Nasi ulam is a popular traditional dish in Kedah. Just like the name suggests, the rice is mixed with the shredded ulam and paired it with keladi masak asam as side dish. Unlike nasi kerabu from Kelantan, nasi ulam is white in colour.
