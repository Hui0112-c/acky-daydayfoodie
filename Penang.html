Penang Hokkien Mee, also known as prawn noodles, is a flavorful noodle soup dish characterized by its rich, prawn-based broth and a combination of yellow egg noodles and rice vermicelli.

Mix of yellow Hokkien wheat noodles and thin rice vermicelli is characterised by its trademark milky-white, salty-savoury soup. A standard bowl will be garnished with shrimps, blood cockles, strips of cuttlefish, and tofu puffs.

Yam Rice

Yam rice is known for its flavorful and hearty combination of yam (taro) and rice. Yam rice is often enjoyed on its own or paired with a savory soup, such as pork soup with pork offal, or a side of vegetables, creating a balanced and satisfying meal.
