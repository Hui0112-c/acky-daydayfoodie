Did You Know? 🍴

🌶️ Malaysia has over 50 unique street dishes per state!
🥥 Coconut milk is a staple ingredient in many desserts.
🍜 Penang is often called the "food capital" of Malaysia.
🍍 Exotic fruits are widely used in traditional snacks.
🥟 Street food vendors often pass down recipes for generations.
🍹 Tropical drinks like cendol and sugarcane juice are must-tries!
